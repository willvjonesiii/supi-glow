Meet Pilar De La Cruz

Her Story

A Journey of Transformation

Pilar is a Dominican woman raised in the Bronx. She is a mother of four amazing children, advocate for children with special needs, certified yoga instructor, entrepreneur and soul connector.

One of Pilar’s biggest motivations has been her daughter, a unique and vivacious special needs young woman who taught her to find strength and balance in life. Her experiences have led her on a journey of self discovery that resulted in heightened self awareness and empowerment which she now utilizes to propel herself and other women.

Pilar is passionate about being a role model for diverse and fierce young women, with the intention to inspire, empower and connect, to create a more conscious and healing journey to self. Leading to loving, compassionate and internal peace.

In addition to Pilar’s practice, her line of all natural body soothing treatments offers delicately handcrafted and Reiki infused chakra tea baths that can be used for healing, relaxation, and remedy.

The Practice

Founded in 2013

Founded in 2013 by soulful Latina wellness coach Pilar De La Cruz, Supi Glow embodies her extraordinary healing journey, triumphing over challenges from teen motherhood to fully autonomous and empowered woman. Choosing transformation over stagnant compliance, Pilar embarked on a lifelong healing journey.

For Pilar De La Cruz, a Reiki certified practitioner and yoga instructor, searching within yourself is the preeminent place to discover true harmony. Pilar began her journey to serenity over five years ago, in search of a remedy that would help her better cope with personal stress and challenging emotions in a healthy, positive, and rewarding way.

Pilar received her instructor certification from ISHTA Yoga, a leading yoga and meditation studio located in Soho, New York. She is also a certified Reiki practitioner—a Japanese therapy technique that incorporates light motions of the hands on and over the body, utilized to reduce stress and encourage relaxation of the mind, body, and soul.

With SupiGlow, Pilar is excited to provide people with the proper guidance and support needed to make the most of their road to self-healing, self-peace and self-love.
