Her Story

Meet Pilar De La Cruz

Founded in Love,
Built on Healing.

Founded in 2013 by soulful Latina wellness coach Pilar De La Cruz, Supi Glow embodies her extraordinary healing journey—triumphing over challenges from teen motherhood to fully autonomous and empowered woman. Choosing transformation over stagnant compliance, Pilar embarked on a lifelong healing journey.

Pilar is passionate about being a role model for diverse and fierce young women, with the intention to inspire, empower and connect, to create a more conscious and healing journey to self. Leading to loving, compassionate and internal peace.
